Welcome! I am currently an Associate Researcher at Beijing Zhongguancun Laboratory, working closely with Dr. Li Chen and Prof. Dan Li. Before that, I received my Ph.D. in Computer Science from Tsinghua University in 2022.

My current research topics include distributed deep learning system, and network security. Besides, I am leading the development of an Internet measurement platform, namely KI3 (Key Information of Internet Infrastructure). I am happy to connect and exchange insights about these topics.

⭐️ I’m looking for self-motivated undergraduate and graduate students who are interested in research on computer network and system. Please feel free to send your resume to my emailbox.
